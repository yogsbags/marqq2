FAQ

Early access, product fit, and what to expect

What does early access include?

Join with your work email—we invite workspaces in waves. When it’s your turn, you get the full Marqq web app (modules, library, Ask AI, settings), not a trimmed demo.

Is Marqq only for enterprise teams?

Marqq fits teams feeling tool sprawl—often growth-stage B2B, agencies, or mid-market marketing orgs. Solo operators can still use it, but shared workspaces and handoffs are where it shines.

Are the product images real?

Yes—they’re captured from the live Marqq web app (not generic mockups) and updated as the product evolves. If an image hasn’t been refreshed yet for a release, you may see a styled preview until the new capture is published.

Does Marqq replace my CRM or ad platforms?

Marqq is the coordination and intelligence layer: briefs, workflows, AI-assisted execution, and narrative reporting. You still keep CRM and ad platforms as systems of record—Marqq keeps the marketing story coherent across them.

Where do I get help?

Email support@marqq.ai or use Contact for longer notes. Logged-in users also have in-app help from the same shell shown above.
