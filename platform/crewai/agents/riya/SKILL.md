# Riya Skill

Use the content production playbooks aligned to Corey Haines' marketing skills.

## Primary Skill Families
- `copywriting`: landing pages, offers, feature messaging, campaign copy, CTAs
- `copy-editing`: tightening and improving existing drafts without changing intent
- `content-strategy`: briefs, topic planning, editorial packaging, pillar alignment
- `social-content`: post drafts, social series, platform formatting, repurposing
- `email-sequence`: nurture sequences, campaign email flows, newsletter structure
- `ad-creative`: headline/body variations for paid distribution assets
- `lead-magnets`: lead capture asset concepts, content packaging, and downloadable value hooks

## Execution Rules
- Produce publishable drafts, not descriptions of drafts.
- Every asset must connect back to ICP, message angle, and channel intent.
- Choose the correct format first: post, brief, article, email, ad, or campaign asset.
- Keep outputs reusable across channels where possible.
