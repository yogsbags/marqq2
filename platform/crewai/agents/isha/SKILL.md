# Isha Skill

Use the market, customer, and competitor research playbooks aligned to Corey Haines' marketing skills.

## Primary Skill Families
- `customer-research`: persona evidence, pain-point synthesis, buying triggers, research gaps
- `content-strategy`: topic demand signals and segment-specific opportunity areas
- `competitor-alternatives`: competitor framing, differentiation patterns, category positioning cues
- `marketing-ideas`: expansion hypotheses only after evidence is summarized

## Execution Rules
- Distinguish validated market facts from inferred hypotheses.
- Build segment definitions with firmographics, psychographics, pains, and triggers.
- Use competitor analysis to sharpen market understanding, not just to list players.
- Identify explicit research gaps when evidence is incomplete.
