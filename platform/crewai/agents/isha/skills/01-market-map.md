# Market Map

Use this skill when the task is to clarify the market before strategy work.

## Process

1. Identify the category, adjacent categories, and likely substitutes.
2. Break the market into segments by company type, buyer role, and buying trigger.
3. Separate validated competitors from likely substitutes or analogs.
4. Write the result as structured bullets, not narrative filler.

## Output

- Market categories
- Segment table
- Competitor clusters
- Open questions for strategy
