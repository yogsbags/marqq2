# CRO Auditor

Use this skill when the user wants to improve conversion rate on a landing page, pricing page, signup form, or any page with a primary CTA.

Tara can audit from: a URL (if crawlable), a screenshot, a copy paste of the page text, or a verbal description.

## Audit Framework

Run all 8 checks in order. Score each 1–10. Average for the overall CRO Score.

1. **Above-the-fold clarity** — Can a first-time visitor understand what this is, who it's for, and what to do next within 5 seconds?
2. **Value proposition strength** — Is the primary benefit specific and differentiated, or generic ("save time, make money")?
3. **Social proof placement and specificity** — Is proof visible without scrolling? Are testimonials specific (numbers, names, outcomes) or vague?
4. **CTA prominence, copy, and friction** — Is the primary CTA visible? Does the button copy state an outcome ("Get my free audit") or just an action ("Submit")?
5. **Form length and field necessity** — Is every field earning its keep? Phone number on a top-of-funnel form is a conversion killer.
6. **Page speed signals** — Heavy hero videos, unoptimized images, multiple third-party scripts. Flag if visible from page inspection.
7. **Mobile experience** — Does the layout collapse correctly? Is the CTA reachable with a thumb on mobile without scrolling?
8. **Trust signals** — Security badges, client logos, guarantee copy, privacy reassurance near form fields.

## Process

1. Run all 8 checks and score each.
2. Separate findings into: Critical Issues (score 1–4), Quick Wins (score 5–6), Already Good (score 7–10).
3. For Critical Issues and Quick Wins, provide an exact recommended fix — not "improve the headline," but a rewritten headline.
4. For hero, CTA, and form specifically — always provide an exact rewrite if they score below 8.

## Output

Use this structure:

---

**Overall CRO Score**: [X]/10   **Biggest drag**: [the one thing hurting CVR most]

### Critical Issues (fix first)

| 1 | ... | "..." | "..." | +X–Y% |

### Quick Wins (do this week)
| # | Check | Current State | Recommended Fix | Estimated CVR Impact |
|---|-------|--------------|-----------------|---------------------|

### Already Working Well
[List the checks scoring 7+ — brief, 1 line each]

### Rewrite Suggestions
**Hero headline**: [exact replacement copy]
**Hero subhead**: [exact replacement copy]
**Primary CTA button**: [exact replacement copy]

---

Do not pad the audit with generic CRO theory. Every finding must be specific to the page being reviewed.
