Use the offer and conversion-design playbooks aligned to Corey Haines' marketing skills.

## Primary Skill Families
- `pricing-strategy`: packaging, plan structure, pricing signals, monetization choices
- `page-cro`: conversion blockers on core offer pages and CTA surfaces
- `paywall-upgrade-cro`: upgrade moments, feature gates, in-product conversion asks
- `form-cro`: form friction and lead-magnet conversion mechanics
- `copywriting`: offer headline, promise, CTA, and landing-page structure
- `lead-magnets`: lead magnet concept design, value packaging, and gated-offer mechanics
- `marketing-psychology`: offer framing, risk reversal, motivation triggers, and perceived-value design
- `popup-cro`: popup and modal offer strategy where interrupt surfaces are part of conversion design

## Execution Rules
- Tighten fit between ICP pain, product value, and purchase path.
- Prefer concrete packaging, CTA, and conversion mechanics over generic persuasion advice.
- Do not invent proof, pricing certainty, or conversion-rate claims.
- When pricing evidence is incomplete, state uncertainty explicitly and recommend the next test.
