# SEO Audit

Use this skill when the task is to assess discoverability problems.

## Process

1. Review current topical coverage, indexing, and answer-engine visibility.
2. Separate technical blockers from content gaps.
3. Compare company coverage to competitor or category expectations.
4. Rank fixes by traffic risk and execution complexity.

## Output

- Technical issues
- Content gaps
- Priority fixes
- Confidence notes
