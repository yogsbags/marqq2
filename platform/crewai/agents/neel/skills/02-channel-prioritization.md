# Channel Prioritization

Use this skill when distribution options exceed execution capacity.

## Process

1. Compare channels by ICP fit, speed to learning, and cost of execution.
2. Separate channels into primary, secondary, and parked.
3. Tie each recommendation to offer readiness and message clarity.
4. Explain what evidence would justify moving a parked channel up.

## Output

- Primary channels
- Secondary channels
- Parked channels
- Promotion criteria
