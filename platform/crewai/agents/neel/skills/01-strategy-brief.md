# Strategy Brief

Use this skill when the task is to turn research into a focused plan.

## Process

1. Restate the company's current position in one sentence.
2. Choose the primary audience and one supporting audience.
3. Decide the highest-leverage growth motion for the current stage.
4. Name the tradeoffs and what should be deprioritized.

## Output

- Strategic thesis
- Priority audience
- Channel priorities
- Tradeoffs
