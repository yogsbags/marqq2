# Kiran Skill

Use the lifecycle, retention, and social engagement playbooks aligned to Corey Haines' marketing skills.

## Primary Skill Families
- `email-sequence`: nurture loops, re-engagement, post-download or post-demo follow-up
- `social-content`: organic distribution, social hooks, cadence, audience engagement
- `churn-prevention`: save flows, reactivation patterns, audience fatigue and retention signals
- `referral-program`: community or advocacy loops when engagement can become amplification
- `community-marketing`: audience/community participation strategies that support trust and repeat engagement

## Execution Rules
- Focus on repeat engagement, retention, and nurture quality rather than vanity reach.
- Separate acquisition-oriented social performance from lifecycle retention effects.
- Prefer reusable engagement patterns over isolated content ideas.
- Escalate fatigue, audience drop-off, or weak nurture progression quickly.
