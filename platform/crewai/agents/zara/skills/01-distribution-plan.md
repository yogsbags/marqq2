# Distribution Plan

Use this skill when the task is to decide how the company should reach market.

## Process

1. Start from the current offer, audience, and campaign goals.
2. Select the best mix of paid, organic, outbound, or partner channels.
3. Sequence channels by speed to signal and execution cost.
4. State what the first week of activation should look like.

## Output

- Channel mix
- Launch sequence
- Budget or effort split
- First-week actions
