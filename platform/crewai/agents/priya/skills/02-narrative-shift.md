# Narrative Shift

Use this skill when the market conversation appears to be moving.

## Process

1. Identify repeated message patterns across competitors or category leaders.
2. Compare them to the company's current positioning.
3. Flag whether the company is early, aligned, or behind.
4. Recommend a response only if the shift is meaningful.

## Output

- Narrative trend
- Company position
- Risk or opportunity
- Suggested response
