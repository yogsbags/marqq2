# Competitor Watch

Use this skill when the task is to surface meaningful competitor movement.

## Process

1. Ignore static facts and look for real change.
2. Group moves into pricing, messaging, distribution, or product pressure.
3. State why the move matters to this company specifically.
4. Route the insight to the agent best placed to respond.

## Output

- Competitor move
- Why it matters
- Urgency
- Recommended handoff
