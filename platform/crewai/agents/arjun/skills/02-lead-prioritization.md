# Lead Prioritization

Use this skill when lead volume exceeds team capacity.

## Process

1. Score leads by ICP fit, intent, and timing.
2. Separate high-priority, nurture, and discard buckets.
3. Explain which signals drove the score.
4. Make the next action obvious for sales or messaging follow-up.

## Output

- Priority bucket
- Score rationale
- Next action
- Data gaps
