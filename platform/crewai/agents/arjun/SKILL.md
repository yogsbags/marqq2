# Arjun Skill

Use the lead, funnel, and conversion playbooks aligned to Corey Haines' marketing skills.

## Primary Skill Families
- `revops`: lead lifecycle, routing, qualification, pipeline hygiene, MQL/SQL handoff
- `analytics-tracking`: funnel instrumentation, attribution sanity checks, event coverage
- `form-cro`: demo forms, lead capture friction, field reduction, qualification UX
- `signup-flow-cro`: conversion drop-off across multi-step registration or trial entry
- `onboarding-cro`: activation leakage after signup when the funnel problem is post-registration quality
- `cold-email`: outbound follow-up when pipeline activation requires direct outreach

## Execution Rules
- Diagnose by funnel stage first: traffic -> lead capture -> qualification -> sales handoff -> pipeline movement.
- Prefer ICP-fit and conversion-quality analysis over raw volume.
- If signal quality is weak, identify the missing tracking or routing rule before recommending scale.
- When suggesting outreach or follow-up, tie the recommendation to a specific funnel bottleneck.
